Pest Control Strategies

Pest and Diseases of Crops

Crop pests are organisms like insects (aphids, caterpillars), mites, nematodes, and rodents that directly damage or consume crops, while crop diseases are caused by pathogens such as fungi, bacteria, and viruses that disrupt normal plant functions. More

Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

IPM is a comprehensive, sustainable approach to controlling pests by combining multiple strategies. Instead of relying on a single method, IPM emphasizes prevention and uses a combination of biological, cultural, physical, and behavioral methods.

More
Tips for Monitoring of Pest Control

Effective pest control monitoring involves regular visual inspections, the use of traps (like sticky or pheromone traps), pest identification, and record-keeping to understand pest biology and track population trends. .
